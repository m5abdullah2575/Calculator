Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate what percentage one number is of another?

Use the formula: (Part ÷ Whole) × 100. For example, to find what percentage 25 is of 200: (25 ÷ 200) × 100 = 12.5%

What's the difference between percentage increase and decrease?

Percentage increase shows growth from the original value, while percentage decrease shows reduction. Both use the original value as the base for comparison.

How do I calculate a discount percentage?

Subtract the sale price from the original price, then divide by the original price and multiply by 100. For example: ($100 - $80) ÷ $100 × 100 = 20% discount

Can I use this calculator for tip calculations?

Yes! Enter your bill amount and the tip percentage (like 18% or 20%) to calculate the exact tip amount and total bill including tip.
