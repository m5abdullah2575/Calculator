Tip Calculator

Calculate tip amounts and split bills among multiple people for restaurants, services, and more.

Tip Guidelines

Excellent Service 20-25%
Good Service 18-20%
Average Service 15-18%
Below Average 10-15%
Takeout/Counter 10-15%
Delivery 15-20%

How to Use the Tip Calculator

  1. 1 Enter the total bill amount before tip
  2. 2 Enter the tip percentage or use the quick buttons (10%, 15%, 18%, 20%)
  3. 3 Enter the number of people splitting the bill
  4. 4 Click "Calculate Tip" to see the tip amount, total, and amount per person
