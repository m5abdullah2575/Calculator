Simple Interest Calculator

Calculate simple interest earned on investments or paid on loans using the formula: SI = P × R × T / 100.

Simple Interest Formula

SI = P × R × T ÷ 100
SI = Simple Interest
P = Principal Amount
R = Rate of Interest (% per annum)
T = Time Period (in years)

Total Amount = Principal + Simple Interest

How to Use the Simple Interest Calculator

  1. 1 Enter the principal amount (initial sum of money)
  2. 2 Enter the annual interest rate as a percentage
  3. 3 Enter the time period in years
  4. 4 Click "Calculate Simple Interest" to get the interest amount and total
