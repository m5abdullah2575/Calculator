Compound Interest Calculator

Calculate compound interest with different compounding frequencies to see how your investments grow over time.

Compound Interest Formula

A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)
A = Final Amount
P = Principal Amount
r = Annual Interest Rate (decimal)
n = Number of times interest is compounded per year
t = Time (in years)

Compound Interest = Final Amount - Principal

How to Use the Compound Interest Calculator

  1. 1 Enter the principal amount (initial investment)
  2. 2 Enter the annual interest rate as a percentage
  3. 3 Enter the time period in years
  4. 4 Select how often the interest is compounded
  5. 5 Click "Calculate Compound Interest" to see your investment growth
